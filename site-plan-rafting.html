Overview

Purpose

Our website will help promote our whitewater rafting tours, provide details about our services, and make it easy for users to book an adventure online. It will also share safety info and build excitement about exploring the great outdoors with us.

Audience

We are targeting adventure seekers, outdoor lovers, and families looking for a fun vacation activity in Colorado. Visitors may range from first-time rafters to experienced thrill-seekers, so our content should be both exciting and informative.
